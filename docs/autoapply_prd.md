# Product Requirements Document (PRD) --- AutoApply AI

## 1. Overview

AutoApply AI is a web-based SaaS platform that automates job discovery,
resume tailoring, and job applications on behalf of users with full
transparency and role-based governance.

## 2. Target Users

-   Job Seekers
-   Career Coaches
-   Placement Agencies
-   Power Users

## 3. Core Features

### Authentication

-   Email/Password
-   Google OAuth
-   Password reset
-   Email verification

### Role-Based Access Control

Roles: Super Admin, Admin, Manager, User

### Resume Management

-   Upload master resume
-   AI-tailored versions per job
-   Version history

### Job Discovery

Sources: - LinkedIn - Indeed - Wellfound - Company career pages

### AI Matching

Score jobs from 0--10 and auto-apply for 7+.

### Automation Scheduler

Daily scheduling with platform filters and job limits.

### Dashboard

-   Jobs applied today
-   Total applications
-   Success rate
-   Platform analytics

### Notifications

Daily summary emails and failure alerts.
